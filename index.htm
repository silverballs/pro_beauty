THE PROFESSIONAL BEAUTY MARKETPLACE

Starting a new business in the professional beauty industry may be a challenging and often high-risk venture. Professional Beauty Consultants, LLC will help guide you through the particulars of our industry by helping you understand all the components of this business and how they operate. Putting together all the pieces is essential to your success.

The Professional Beauty field is comprised of salons, distributors, spas, beauty stores, beauty schools, and beauty chains. According to industry insiders, there are an estimated over 250,000 salons (hair, nails and/or skin) operating in the United States today, employing well over two million licensed cosmetologists. These numbers indicate that the professional beauty industry is vibrant and has maintained a large presence in today’s business world. Within our rapidly changing market there are many challenges that companies have to face in doing business in our industry. With proper guidance and advice, the prospects for new companies are excellent.

You need Professional Beauty Consultants, LLC in order to get off to the best possible start, the very first time.
